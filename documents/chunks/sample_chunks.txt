Source: LuxeLife Dining - Livingstone Meal Plans
File: 11_meal_plans.txt
URL: https://www.luxelifedining.com/livingstone-mealplans
Source type: Dining / meal plan information
Word count: 157

The LuxeLife Dining meal plan page for Livingstone College describes resident and commuter meal plans. It says meal plans can be used at the all-you-care-to-eat City Market or City Market Takeout, with dining dollars usable in retail locations. The page says resident students can sign up for a meal plan through Auxiliary Services on the second floor of the Student Service Center. The resident option shown is a 19-meal plan, which gives students 19 meals per week in the dining hall from Monday through Sunday. The commuter meal plan section says commuter plans are meant to be convenient and budget friendly, are valid for one semester, and are non-transferable. The FAQ says students may upgrade their meal plan during the first two weeks of the semester, unused meals do not carry over week to week, refunds are not given for leftover meals, and meal plans cannot be used to treat a guest during the same meal period.
