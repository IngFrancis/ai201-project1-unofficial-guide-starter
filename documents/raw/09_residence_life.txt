Title: Livingstone College Residence Life
URL: https://livingstone.edu/students/residence-life/
Source type: Official housing / residence life information

Text:
Livingstone College's Residence Life page says the Residence Life office is located in the W.J. Walls Center on the second floor at 800 W. Thomas Street in Salisbury, North Carolina. The page lists an on-call phone number and identifies the Dean of Residence Life. The Residence Life section explains that campus housing is connected to student social development and that the college wants students to live, learn, and mature in an environment that supports self-respect, self-direction, social dignity, and academic progress. Students must be admitted to the college and pay required non-refundable prepayments before a housing application is accepted. The housing application must be submitted each year. Accepting a housing assignment means the student agrees to the terms and conditions of the assignment, and failure to follow residence life rules may lead to loss of housing privileges. Residence halls are managed by hall directors, supervisors, and resident assistants.
