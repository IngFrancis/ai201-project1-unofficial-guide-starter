Title: Niche - Livingstone College Overview
URL: https://www.niche.com/colleges/livingstone-college/
Source type: College overview / student survey data

Text:
Niche describes Livingstone College as a private, Methodist, historically Black college located in Salisbury, North Carolina. The page gives Livingstone an overall grade of C and reports a rating of 3.38 out of 5 based on 615 reviews. It lists the acceptance rate as 59 percent and the total undergraduate enrollment as 924 students. Niche describes Livingstone as a small institution with a full-time undergraduate enrollment of 909 and part-time undergraduate enrollment of 15. Popular majors listed include Business, Sport and Fitness Management, and Biology. Niche also shows category grades such as Diversity B, Athletics B, Party Scene B+, Professors C+, Dorms B minus, Campus Food C, Student Life C+, and Safety C+. This source is useful for general questions about Livingstone's size, location, student experience, academics, cost, and campus quality.
