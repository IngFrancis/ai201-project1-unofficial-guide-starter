Title: LuxeLife Dining - Livingstone Dining Menu
URL: https://www.luxelifedining.com/livingstone-menu
Source type: Dining menu / food options

Text:
The Livingstone dining menu page did not load usable menu text during collection, so this document records dining-related information from accessible dining and campus-food sources. LuxeLife Dining says Livingstone meal plans can be used at City Market or City Market Takeout, and describes the dining hall as an all-you-care-to-eat location with comfort foods, pizza, grilled sandwiches, and vegetarian options. It also says dining dollars or bonus points may be used in retail locations. Niche's campus life page gives Livingstone campus food a C grade and reports that meal plans are available. Student poll responses on Niche mention food options or nearby options such as Naples Cafe, Sonic, Italy Cafe, Thelma's, the cafe, Cook Out, McDonald's, and other local food places. This source is useful for questions about what food options students mention, but it may not answer questions about the exact menu for a specific day.
