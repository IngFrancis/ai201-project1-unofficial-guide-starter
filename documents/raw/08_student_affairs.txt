Title: Livingstone College Student Affairs
URL: https://livingstone.edu/students/
Source type: Official student resources

Text:
Livingstone College's Student Affairs page says the Office of Student Affairs is located in the W.J. Walls Center on the third floor at 800 W. Thomas Street in Salisbury, North Carolina. The office hours are Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. The page lists student-facing areas such as Residence Life, Health Services, Student Activities, Counseling Services, Career Readiness Institute, Success Center, and Spiritual Life. The mission text says Student Affairs supports students from orientation to graduation through programs, services, activities, resources, advocacy, problem resolution, and crisis intervention. It also says Student Affairs helps students apply classroom learning to real-life practice through student organizations, service learning, and leadership development. This source is useful for questions about where students can go for support, campus resources, leadership opportunities, and student problem solving.
