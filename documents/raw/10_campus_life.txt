Title: Livingstone College Campus Life
URL: https://livingstone.edu/campus-life/
Source type: Official campus life information

Text:
Livingstone College's official Campus Life page presents campus life as a mix of friendship, activities, housing, Greek life, learning communities, food, and local Salisbury experiences. The page says there are clubs and activities that can become both resume highlights and lifelong memories. It links students to clubs and activities, housing information, Greek life, and learning communities. The page also highlights food and says students should explore Livingstone's Culinary Arts program options. It includes a section about exploring Livingstone and the surrounding area, mentioning local attractions such as Piedmont Players Theatre and Piedmont Skydiving. This source is useful for questions about what students can do outside class, how the college describes involvement, and what official campus life resources are available.
