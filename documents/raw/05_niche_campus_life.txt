Title: Niche - Livingstone College Campus Life
URL: https://www.niche.com/colleges/livingstone-college/campus-life/
Source type: Campus life / student polls

Text:
Niche's campus life page gives Livingstone College a Party Scene grade of B+, Safety grade of C+, Dorms grade of B minus, and Campus Food grade of C. The page says on-campus housing is available and that freshmen are not required to live on campus, although 93 percent of freshmen live on campus. It lists the average housing cost as ,262 per year and the average meal plan cost as ,531 per year. Student polls on the page say 36 percent of students rate overall dorm quality as great and 45 percent say the dorm social atmosphere is great. For campus community, 25 percent say they love everything about the school and have a lot of campus pride, while 53 percent say they like mostly everything but wish some things were different. Food poll responses mention options such as Naples Cafe, Sonic, Italy Cafe, Thelma's, and the cafe.
