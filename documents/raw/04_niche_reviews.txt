Title: Niche - Livingstone College Reviews
URL: https://www.niche.com/colleges/livingstone-college/reviews/
Source type: Student reviews

Text:
The Niche reviews page summarizes student opinions about Livingstone College. The page shows an overall review rating of about 3.38 out of 5 based on 615 reviews. Niche's student review summary says students often describe Livingstone as close-knit, supportive, and helpful for academic and personal growth. Reviews mention approachable professors and staff, smaller class sizes, school pride, diversity, and a welcoming environment. At the same time, students also mention areas that could improve, including campus organization, communication, facilities, dining options, academic and career resources, student activities, transportation, and housing. Some reviews say the college helped students grow and build relationships, while others say students need to stay proactive and advocate for themselves. This source is useful for questions about student satisfaction, strengths, weaknesses, and common student complaints.
