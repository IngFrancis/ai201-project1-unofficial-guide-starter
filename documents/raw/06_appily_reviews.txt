Title: Appily - Livingstone College Reviews
URL: https://www.appily.com/colleges/livingstone-college/reviews
Source type: Student reviews / college profile

Text:
Appily lists Livingstone College as a private four-year college located in Salisbury, North Carolina. Its review page includes student comments about the college experience. One visible review describes Livingstone as a good HBCU and mentions basketball opportunities. Another student review says Livingstone has a student-friendly campus environment with staff and professors who are willing to help students reach their academic goals. Another review says attending Livingstone taught the student about college and life in general, not only academics. This source is useful because it gives another student-review perspective outside of Niche. It can help answer questions about whether students feel supported, how students describe the environment, and what kind of personal growth students connect with the school.
